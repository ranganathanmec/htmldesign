About Us

Information Technology has touched every part of the economy and has become an indispensable tool for commercial use research, education and personal use.

  • Change Technologies set foot into this developing technology area in the year 1998 to provide value based solutions. Since then Change technologies has been making ahead into consistent vertical performance growth in pursuit of excellence in field of selling branded PCs, Laptops, Servers, Printers, Networking Products, UPS Systems and other peripheral products.
  • We are equipped into a dedicated team of skilled manpower to deliver solutions for all your hardware requirements. We work on a client centre approach and are committed to customer relationship management and customer satisfaction. We believe that our relation does not end with just selling but continue to provide quick solutions for all their hardware and software requirements.
